Chicken

Chickens are average-sized fowls, characterized by smaller heads, short beaks and wings, and a round body perched on featherless legs. Exact size varies greatly among breeds, as does color. In many breeds, both sexes will have fleshy skin folds on the chin and atop the head, known as wattles and combs, respectively.

Mutton

Mutton is meat from a mature sheep, usually between two and three years old. It has a stronger flavor and denser, tougher texture than lamb because the animal is older and contains more fat and muscle. Mutton is also relatively fatty, with a deep red color and a strong gamey flavor.

Prawns

Prawn Curry (Prawn Masala): This simple recipe uses ground spices and can include sauted prawns. Indian Prawn Curry: This recipe uses garam masala, red chili powder, ground coriander, and ground cumin. Tomato paste adds a rich, tangy flavor. Traditional Goan Shrimp Curry: This spicy, tangy curry uses coconut milk and a variety of spices. Some recipes use tamarind as a souring agent, while others use kokum.

Fish

Fish curry is a dish that features white fish cooked in a spiced broth or sauce. It's a staple in many Asian countries and is made in different ways using locally available ingredients.
